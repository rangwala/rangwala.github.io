Welcome
I am Huzefa Rangwala, an Associate Professor at the Department of Computer Science & Engineering at the George Mason University in the Northern Virginia Area and a member of the Bioengineering Initiative.
Latest News
Research Interests:
My research interests include the areas of data mining and inter-disciplinary applications in the areas of learning sciences, bioinformatics and bioengineering.

There is an emphasis on development of novel algorithms, and engineering of effective solutions. My research has resulted in development in useful and efficient software tools that aid biologists to make key discoveries, and some of the contributions have advanced the field of computer science. Please refer to the publications page of my web-site for learning more about my research.
