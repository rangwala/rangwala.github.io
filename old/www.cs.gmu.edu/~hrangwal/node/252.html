Co-organizing Knowledge Discovery in Health Care & Medicine workshop at ECML 2011

05 Apr 2011
Posted by rangwala

Our workshop, Knowledge Discovery in Health Care and Medicine (KD-HCM) was approved by the ECML/PKDD 2011 organizers and will be held on September 9, 2011 in Athens, Greece. Please consider submitting your research here.

Here is a link the web-site and CFP: http://www.cs.gmu.edu/~hrangwal/kd-hcm

Email the co-chairs at kdhcmchairs2011@gmail.com

Co-Chairs:

  • Huzefa Rangwala, George Mason University, US.

  • Andrea Tagarelli, University of Calabria, IT.

  • Nikil Wale, Pfizer, US.

  • George Karypis, University of Minnesota, US.
