More Awards for Eric Tao (TJHSST)

09 Apr 2012
Posted by rangwala

More Accolades for Eric & Marvin!

  • Won $1000 scholarship for participating in the Siemens Science Fair Regional Competition at Georgia Tech University.
  • Received honorable mention for submission to Poster on the Hill 2012 event.
  • Participated in the local Intel Science and Engineering Fair 2012 held in Fairfax, VA. Best team in Computer Science and $800 award from Northrop Grumman
  • Participated at the state-level Intel Science & Engineering Fair 2012 held in Norfolk, VA. First place in Computer Science category and won $250 award.
    • Congrats to Eric (Ye) Tao and Marvin Qian, high school students at Thomas Jefferson High School for Science & Technology who are in the Regional Finals for the Siemens Competition.

    The team is mentored at the Computer Science Department, George Mason University under the guidance of Huzefa Rangwala. The project was related to the modeling of solenoid proteins using machine learning approaches.

    They will compete at the regional event held at Georgia Tech University (Nov 3-5, 2011) for a place in the national competition.

    From the competition web-site & FCPS News Release:

    http://www.siemens-foundation.org/en/competition.htm

    http://commweb.fcps.edu/newsreleases/newsrelease.cfm?newsid=1843


    "Through the Siemens Competition in Math, Science, and Technology, students have an opportunity to achieve national recognition for science research projects that they complete in high school. The competition is administered by the College Board and funded by the Siemens Foundation.


    A record number of students—2,436—registered to enter the 2011 competition, submitting 1,541 projects. "
